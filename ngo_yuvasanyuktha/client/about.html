WHO WE ARE?

About Us

Yuva Sanyuktha is a charitable organisation that originated from the collective vision of medical students.

Driven by the desire to make a positive impact, the organisation focuses on providing assistance and support to children facing various needs and challenges.

Our Core Values

Stewardship

We strive in making responsible decisions that align with our values and contribute to our mission.

Humility

We as an organization pursue to remain grounded and embrace a mindset of continuous growth.

Team Work

We believe in teamwork, it involves effective communication, mutual support and leveraging each team member's strengths to achieve shared objectives.

Compassion

Our utmost priority is to reach out to those in need with care and compassion, striving to listen actively and offer support.

Transparency

Our aim is to build trust and credibility.
