Privacy Policy for Yuva Sanyuktha Foundation

This Privacy Policy describes how Yuva Sanyuktha Foundation ("we," "us," or "our") collects, uses, and discloses your personal information when you visit our website (yuvasanyktha.co.in) (the "Site") or otherwise interact with us.

Information We Collect

Personal Information: This can include information that can be used to identify an individual, such as name, email address, postal address, phone number, and donation history. We typically collect this information when you donate, sign up for our newsletter, register for an event, or contact us.

Non-Personal Information: This includes information that cannot be used to identify an individual, such as browsing habits, IP address, operating system, and device type. We collect this information automatically through cookies and other tracking technologies.

How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect for various purposes, including:

  • To process your donations and keep you informed about your contributions.
  • To send you updates about our organization, programs, and events.
  • To analyze and improve our Site and services.
  • To comply with legal and regulatory requirements.

Sharing Your Information

We will not share your information with any third-party service providers. Our service provider is obligated to keep your information confidential and use it only for the purposes we have specified.

We will not sell or rent your personal information to third parties. We may disclose your information if required by law or in the good faith belief that such action is necessary to conform to applicable laws or legal processes served on us, or to protect and defend the rights or property of Yuva Sanyuktha Foundation or its supporters.

Data Retention

We will retain your personal information for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes for which it was collected, unless a longer retention period is required or permitted by law.

Security

We take reasonable steps to protect your information from unauthorized access, disclosure, alteration, or destruction. However, no internet or electronic storage system is 100% secure.

Children's Privacy

Our Site is not directed to children under the age of 18. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 18. If you are a parent or guardian and you believe your child has below 18 provided us with personal information, please contact us.

Changes to this Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will post any changes on this page.
